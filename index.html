07 — FAQ

Common questions

Is parking available at all locations?

Yes, complimentary secure parking is available at all three locations — Yercaud, Kodaikanal, and Ooty.

Do you allow campfires at the cottages?

Absolutely. Each cottage includes a private campfire area. Firewood is provided and staff will help you light it safely.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged on request.

Are pets allowed?

Yes! We are a pet-friendly resort. Please inform us in advance so we can prepare accordingly.

Is there a restaurant on-site?

Yes, we have a farm-to-table restaurant serving local Tamil Nadu cuisine and continental dishes.

How do I make a reservation?

You can book through our website's booking page, call us at +91 9999999999, or WhatsApp us directly.
