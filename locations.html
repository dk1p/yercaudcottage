Choose your escape

Four breathtaking destinations across Tamil Nadu and the Nilgiris.

Hill Station · Salem District

Yercaud

Nestled in the Shevaroy Hills at 1,515 meters, Yercaud is a serene hill station known for its coffee and orange plantations, dense forests, and the beautiful Emerald Lake. Far less crowded than Ooty or Kodaikanal, it offers a genuine escape.

Altitude1,515 meters
Best SeasonMarch – June, Oct – Dec
Distance from Chennai~350 km · 5 hrs
Our Cottages3 properties, 12 rooms

Highlights: Shevaroy Hills, Emerald Lake, Anna Park, Rose Garden, Coffee Plantation Walk, Lady's Seat viewpoint.

Book Yercaud Stay
Princess of Hills · Dindigul District

Kodaikanal

The "Gift of the Forest" — Kodaikanal sits at 2,133 meters in the Palani Hills. Famous for its star-shaped lake, cascading waterfalls, and the rare Neelakurinji flower that blooms once every 12 years.

Altitude2,133 meters
Best SeasonApril – June, Sept – Nov
Distance from Chennai~524 km · 8 hrs
Our Cottages4 properties, 16 rooms

Highlights: Kodaikanal Lake, Coaker's Walk, Silver Cascade, Pine Forest, Dolphin's Nose, Pillar Rocks.

Book Kodaikanal Stay
Queen of Hills · Nilgiris District

Ooty

Udhagamandalam — the "Queen of Hills" — is the capital of the Nilgiris. At 2,240 meters, it's famous for its lush tea gardens, the beloved Nilgiri Mountain Railway (UNESCO World Heritage), and the Government Botanical Garden.

Altitude2,240 meters
Best SeasonYear-round · Peak: Mar–Jun
Distance from Chennai~540 km · 8.5 hrs
Our Cottages5 properties, 20 rooms

Highlights: Ooty Lake, Botanical Garden, Doddabetta Peak, Toy Train, Tea Museum, Mudumalai Wildlife Sanctuary.

Book Ooty Stay
Hidden Gem · Coimbatore District

Valparai

The most secluded of our locations, Valparai is a plateau in the Anaimalai Hills at 1,500 meters. Known for its misty tea and coffee estates, abundant wildlife including elephants and leopards, and dramatic 40 hairpin bends approach.

Altitude1,500 meters
Best SeasonNov – Feb
Distance from Coimbatore~100 km · 3 hrs
Our Cottages2 properties, 8 rooms

Highlights: Anamalai Tiger Reserve, Aliyar Dam, Grass Hills, Tea Factory Tours, Elephant spotting, Misty trekking trails.

Book Valparai Stay
