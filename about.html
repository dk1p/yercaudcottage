Our Story

Born from a love of
nature and hospitality

RENATURE was founded in 2018 by a family of passionate naturalists and hospitality veterans. We started with a single cottage in Yercaud — a converted planter's bungalow overlooking a coffee estate — and fell in love with the idea of sharing these magical landscapes with guests who crave silence, beauty and genuine care.

Today we operate 14 properties across 4 locations, each designed to feel like a private home rather than a hotel. No reception desks. No uniforms. Just warm hosts, extraordinary nature and unforgettable stays.

Our Values

What makes us different

01

Absolute Privacy

Each cottage is a standalone property. No shared walls, no shared hallways — just your private world in the middle of nature.

02

Genuine Hospitality

Our hosts are locals who know every trail, waterfall and viewpoint. They're there when you need them and invisible when you don't.

03

Sustainable Stays

Solar power, rainwater harvesting, composting, farm-to-table food. We protect the nature that makes our properties extraordinary.

04

No Cookie-Cutter Rooms

Every house has a unique story, design and atmosphere. We work with local artisans and use local materials in each property.

05

Pets Welcome

We believe your furry family members deserve a holiday too. All our properties are pet-friendly with outdoor spaces to roam.

06

Flexible Policies

Life happens. We offer free date changes up to 72 hours before arrival and full refunds for cancellations 7+ days out.
